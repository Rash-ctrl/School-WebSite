# Rules & Regulations

General Conduct

  • Students must be respectful to teachers, staff, and peers at all times.
  • Disruptive behavior, bullying, or use of foul language is strictly prohibited.
  • Students must maintain discipline within the school premises.

Attendance & Punctuality

  • Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory to appear for exams.
  • Students must arrive on time and be present for the morning assembly.
  • Late arrivals must report to the office with a valid reason.

Uniform & Appearance

  • Students must wear the complete, clean school uniform daily.
  • Hair must be neatly groomed. No extreme hairstyles or coloring allowed.
  • Wearing ID cards is compulsory inside the campus.

Academic Integrity

  • Cheating in any form during tests or exams is strictly prohibited.
  • Plagiarism in assignments will lead to disciplinary action.
  • All homework and projects must be submitted on time.

Use of Electronic Devices

  • Mobile phones and smart devices are not allowed unless permitted by teachers.
  • Misuse of technology (social media, games) during school hours is banned.

Property & Cleanliness

  • Students are responsible for keeping the school environment clean and tidy.
  • Any damage to school property must be reported and may lead to a fine.
  • Graffiti or defacement of walls and furniture is strictly prohibited.

Disciplinary Action

  • Violation of rules may result in verbal/written warnings, detention, or suspension.
  • Repeated offenses will involve parent meetings and could lead to expulsion.
  • The Principal’s decision will be final in all disciplinary matters.
