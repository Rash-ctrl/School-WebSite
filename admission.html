# ADMISSION PROCESS

Why Choose Navjeevan Adarsh Hr. Sec. School?

  • Experienced & Dedicated Faculty: Passionate educators focused on student development.
  • Modern Facilities: Science labs, computer labs, library, playgrounds, and arts rooms.
  • Balanced Curriculum: Academics with co-curricular excellence.
  • Safe & Positive Environment: A secure, respectful space for all students.
  • Technology-Enabled Learning: Smart classrooms and digital resources.

Admission Process

Step 1: Enquiry & Counseling

Contact us via phone or email to speak with our admission counselors or visit our campus for a tour and discussion.

Step 2: Application Form

Collect or download the admission form and fill in all required details with supporting documents.

Step 3: Submission of Documents

Documents required:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Aadhar Card (Student & Parents)
  • 4 Passport-size Photos
  • Transfer Certificate (Grades 2+)
  • Previous School Report Card
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Address Proof

Step 4: Entrance Test / Interaction

Students may appear for a basic test or interaction depending on their class.

Step 5: Confirmation of Admission

After assessment and verification, admission confirmation and fee details will be shared.

Step 6: Fee Payment & Enrollment

Pay the fee to finalize admission. Admission receipt and student ID will be issued upon payment.

Additional Information

  • Sibling Priority: Siblings of current students are given preference.
  • Limited Seats: Admissions are subject to seat availability.
  • Inclusive Policy: Open to students from all backgrounds.
