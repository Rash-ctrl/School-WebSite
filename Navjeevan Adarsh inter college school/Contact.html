We welcome you to be the part of the family of NAVJEEVAN ADARSH INTER COLLEGE.

Throughout my life, I have been deeply influenced by the value of Culture, heritage of India. NAVJEEVAN ADARSH INTER COLLEGE is a culmination of my vision to be finest education institution adopting the policy of “ Quality in Education” on academics and extracurricular activities on the development of children’s, without discrimination whatsoever on the basis of religion, community, race, ethnicity or gender.
